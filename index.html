About For the Love of R&B

For the Love of R&B was created to bridge the gap between music consumers and the current landscape of the genre of R&B music. This website will be dedicated to highlighting the contributions of mainstream and indie artists, from the rookies to the veterans. By showcasing the diversity and abundance of quality R&B music being released, we hope to every music fan will find what they like!
