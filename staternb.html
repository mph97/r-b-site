Current State of R&B

R&B is Alive & Well!

Despite claims that the genre is dead, some of the strongest projects of the past several years have been from R&B artists. No matter if you're looking in the mainstream or more on the indie side, newcomers and veterans alike have released quality material. that blends elements of traditional R&B instrumentation with modern production and songwriting. Record label support as it was prior to the 2010s has waned, for the most part, which has made it difficult for some R&B lovers to stay up to date on what's new from their favorite artists. Still, if you take the time to check blogs, social media, and playlists, you'll definitely find something to love!
